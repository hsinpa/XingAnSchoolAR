The class-reading room is the tropical zone of the library. There is room for two classes to sit down and enjoy reading together. See the coconut tree at the corner? It's a common tree in the tropical zone.\n
Can you find the pangolin hidden here? Pangolins are also called scaly anteaters. They don't have teeth, but they have very long tongues. When in danger, they curl up and rely on their plate-like scales for protection.
