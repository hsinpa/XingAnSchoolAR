The front desk is the temperate zone of the library. There is also a little stage for story-telling sessions and performances. You can sit or even lie down on the stage to read. There is a maple tree by the front desk, and a birch tree by the stage. Their leaves change color in the fall.\n
Can you find the Formosan black bear hidden here? They are black with a big, white V-shaped mark on their chests. They are also called moon bears because the white mark is like a crescent moon.
